STEP ONE

Meet a Personal Tailor



Call us for Doorstep visit or Meet us at our studio and our expert personal tailors who will guide you through a collection of cloths for every season and occasion.

STEP TWO

Select the fabric & customise the looks

Choose from a wide range of colours, fabrics and finishes. Everything from the cut and lining, to the buttons and embroidery is chosen by you.

STEP THREE

Get Measured



Traditional way of taking Measurments gives precise body measurements and figuration to ensure an exceptional fit. Our garments are cut to fit the individual.

STEP FOUR

FINAL FITTING AND DELIVERY

Garments are completed over the course, at which point you will be invited back in-store or We will visit your home for a fitting. If any minor tweaks are needed then these are done within a few days And will be Ready to Ship or deliver them.
